About

I'm a civil engineer and master's student specializing in structural engineering, with a focus on seismic performance assessment of reinforced concrete structures in Nepal's high-risk seismic zone.

Beyond analysis, I build Python-based tools to automate structural design checks per IS 456 and IS 13920 — bridging the gap between code provisions and practical engineering workflows. I'm multilingual (Nepali, English, Hindi, Maithali) and enjoy teaching as much as building.
